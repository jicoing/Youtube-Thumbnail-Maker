How to Use This Thumbnail Creator

1

Upload Your Image

Either drag and drop an image file onto the upload area or click the "Select File" button to choose an image from your device. The ideal aspect ratio for YouTube thumbnails is 16:9.

2

Customize Your Text

In the "Text" tab, add your main title and optional subheading. Adjust the text color, size, and shadow effect to make your text stand out against the background image.

3

Apply Visual Effects

Switch to the "Effects" tab to enhance your image with visual effects like brightness, contrast, or color adjustments. You can also add a gradient overlay to make text more readable.

4

Add a Border

Use the "Border" tab to frame your thumbnail with a custom border. Choose the color, width, and style that best complements your design.

5

Download Your Thumbnail

Once you're satisfied with your design, click the "Download Thumbnail" button to save the image to your device. The image will be saved in JPEG format with the optimal resolution for YouTube.
